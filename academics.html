Academic Curriculum

Our curriculum is designed to meet the educational standards and holistic development needs of every child. We follow a structured program from Kindergarten to Class 12.

Subjects Offered:

  • English Language & Literature
  • Mathematics
  • Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Social Studies (History, Civics, Geography)
  • Hindi / Sanskrit
  • Computer Science
  • General Knowledge & Moral Education
  • Physical Education
  • Art & Craft / Music

Learning Approach:

We focus on experiential learning through activities, projects, and group discussions. Our teaching methods encourage creativity, critical thinking, and curiosity in every student.
