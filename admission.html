Admission Process

We welcome students from all backgrounds and provide equal opportunity for quality education. Admission is open for Nursery to Class 12.

  • ✔️ Admission form available online and at school office
  • ✔️ Photocopy of birth certificate and previous marksheet required
  • ✔️ Passport-size photo of student (2 copies)
  • ✔️ Entrance test for classes above Grade 1

For queries: Call us at +91-9876543210 or visit the school office (Mon–Sat, 9am–1pm)
